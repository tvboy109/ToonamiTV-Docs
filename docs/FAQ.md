# Frequently Asked Questions

## General Questions

### What is ToonamiTV?
ToonamiTV is a private Roku channel that provides classic animation content in a familiar broadcast format.

### Is this an official Toonami channel?
No, this is a fan-made project for personal use.

### How much does it cost?
The channel is free to install and use.

## Technical Questions

### What internet speed do I need?
We recommend at least 5 Mbps for optimal viewing.

### Which Roku devices are supported?
Any Roku device running Roku OS 9.0 or higher.

### Can I watch on multiple devices?
Yes, you can install the channel on multiple Roku devices.

## Content Questions

### What shows are available?
The channel features classic animation content. Check the EPG for current programming.

### When are shows updated?
The schedule is updated regularly. Check the EPG for the latest schedule.

### Can I request shows?
Currently, we do not take show requests.

## Support Questions

### How do I report issues?
Contact us through official support channels.

### How often is the channel updated?
Updates are released as needed to improve stability and features.

### Where can I get help?
Check our documentation or contact support for assistance.
