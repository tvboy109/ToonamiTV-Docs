# Installing ToonamiTV

## Quick Installation
1. Visit [my.roku.com/account/add](https://my.roku.com/account/add)
2. Enter channel code: **CJXJPGD**
3. Or use this direct link: [Add ToonamiTV to Roku](https://my.roku.com/account/add/CJXJPGD)
4. Wait for the channel to appear on your Roku homescreen

## System Requirements
- Any Roku device running Roku OS 9.0 or higher
- Stable internet connection (5+ Mbps recommended)
- Roku account in good standing

## First-Time Setup
1. Launch ToonamiTV from your Roku home screen
2. Wait for initial channel load
3. Enjoy your favorite shows!

## Troubleshooting

### Channel Not Appearing
1. Verify your Roku is online
2. Try restarting your Roku device:
   - Settings → System → System Restart
3. Re-add the channel if needed

### Playback Issues
- Check your internet connection
- Ensure your Roku device meets requirements
- Try restarting the channel
